Trend Mixer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-28

1. Overview

Trend Mixer provides tools for discovering trends, generating short-form creator assets, and preparing reviewed content for publishing workflows.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers needed for login and app access
  • Creator-authorized platform account information such as display name and avatar
  • Trend URLs and creator-selected content metadata
  • Generated project assets and export settings
  • Basic technical logs used for reliability and abuse prevention

3. TikTok Data

If you connect your TikTok account, we only access data and permissions that you explicitly authorize.

  • Basic account information made available through user.info.basic
  • Creator-authorized posting actions using video.publish or video.upload

We do not use TikTok authorization to post content without a creator action inside the app.

4. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate creators
  • Show connected account information
  • Generate short-form publishing assets
  • Support creator-authorized export and posting flows
  • Improve reliability, safety filtering, and abuse prevention

5. Content Safety

We apply filtering and review rules to reduce unsafe or prohibited content categories, including explicit sexual content, graphic violence, self-harm, and unsafe content involving minors.

6. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate hosting, authentication, storage, analytics, or creator-authorized publishing workflows.

7. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the service, legal compliance, dispute handling, and security.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect tokens, creator project data, and connected platform information.

9. Your Choices

You may disconnect your TikTok account and request deletion of your account data, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.
